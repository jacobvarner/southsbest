Teams

Teams will be redirected to a form to submit a PDF copy of their notebook along with other team info.

Once notebooks have been judged, teams should be able to look back over their notebook scores and see comments left by the judges.


Judges

Judges will be redirected to a login page where they will be granted access to the teams notbooks that they have been assigned to read and score.

Judges can review the notebooks and leave their scores and notes in the system so that teams can see the feedback once judging is complete.
